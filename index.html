Dear Tita Ai, how are you? :)

Hello, Tita Ai, this is your day! You were born today and I wish to celebrate that even if I'm not there beside you.
I made you this simple message to remind you how special you are in my heart, Tita Ai. May the things that bother your tiny fragile heart be at peace when you think about how far you've come...
from a bwisit but reliable teenager I looked up to,
to some grown up individual I know has matured into a beautiful butterfly.

Tita Ai, you've reached this far
and I'm always your little niece who's proud of you.

Thank you so much for the shared fun and so-so memories,
and every little thing that reminds me that life isn't bad at all
because you're there as my tita---our tita.

Tita Ai, I don't think I could ever lose you in my life. Though I'm aware we talk less and less as time goes by, heck
you haven't even cried to me yet about your ex!

But know that I'm still holding onto the idea that we'll grow old together.
like you always used to say.
And I hope it's not only because we're close by age,
but because you'd want me to be there too :')

Happiest 27th, Tita Ai!

Your first niece
that you so dearly loved as a baby
as described by my mother,
Chai
